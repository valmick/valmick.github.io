# Introduction

## Symptoms
1. Behind on Language skills
2. Sometimes doesn't respond to his name
3. When focussed on activity like his mixer grinder he will not respond
4. He likes textures, so for example a book with a velvet texture,
   he will hold it and keep stroking it, he also likes velvet
   bedsheets. 
5. Throwing trantrums or having meltdowns
   1. Take him away from water
   2. Take him away from the sand
   3. Take him away from his mixer grinder
6. Sometimes does a repetitive hand movement like Rajnikant flipping
   his specs
7. He is not friendly to all.. he chooses his favorites and it takes
   him a lot of time to get to know someone to be able to mingle
   with that person
